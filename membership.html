FAQ

Common Questions

What age do you have to be to join?

Our members range from 8 years old upwards, with no upper age limit. We have programmes suitable for children, teenagers, and adults.

Do I need to be a good swimmer to join?

We welcome swimmers of all abilities. Whether you're just building confidence or training for competitions, we have a squad that's right for you.

What if I can't make all the sessions?

We understand that schedules can be busy. Your membership gives you access to all sessions in your tier, and you can attend as many as your schedule allows.

Is there a joining fee?

There's no separate joining fee - just your monthly membership payment. Contact us to discuss specific pricing for your chosen plan.

Can I try before I commit?

Yes! We offer trial sessions so you can experience our training before making a commitment. Contact us to arrange your trial.

What equipment do I need?

You'll need appropriate swimwear, goggles, a towel, and a water bottle. Club kit is available to purchase separately.
