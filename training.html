Our Approach

Training That Works

At Burntwood Swimming Club, we believe in structured, progressive training that adapts to each swimmer's needs. Our qualified coaches create personalised training plans that challenge swimmers while ensuring proper technique development.

Whether you're training for fitness, technique improvement, or competitive success, our sessions are designed to help you achieve your goals in a supportive and motivating environment.

Our Squads

Find Your Squad

We offer training programmes for every level, from beginners to elite competitors.

Beginners

Development Squad

Perfect for swimmers aged 8+ who are building confidence and developing basic stroke techniques. Focus on water safety, technique fundamentals, and building stamina.

  • Ages 8-12
  • Basic stroke development
  • Water confidence building
  • 1-2 sessions per week
Intermediate

Improvers Squad

For swimmers with solid basic technique looking to improve endurance and refine all four competitive strokes. Introduction to training sets and pacing.

  • Ages 10+
  • Stroke refinement
  • Endurance building
  • 2-3 sessions per week
Advanced

Competitive Squad

High-performance training for dedicated swimmers aiming to compete at galas and championships. Intensive coaching with focus on race technique and competition preparation.

  • All ages (ability-based)
  • Competition preparation
  • Advanced technique
  • 3-4 sessions per week
All Ages

Masters Squad

Adult swimmers looking to maintain fitness, improve technique, or return to competitive swimming. Supportive environment with flexible training options.

  • Ages 18+
  • Fitness focused
  • Flexible commitment
  • 1-4 sessions per week
