Our Squads

Find Your Squad

We offer supportive training programmes for every level. Your squad becomes your swimming family.

Confidence Building

Development Squad

Perfect for swimmers aged 8+ building water confidence and learning fundamental techniques. We celebrate every milestone and focus on creating a love for swimming.

  • Ages 8-12
  • Confidence-focused approach
  • Fun, supportive environment
  • 1-2 sessions per week
Progress Focused

Improvers Squad

For swimmers ready to develop stronger techniques and build endurance. Track your personal progress and celebrate your improvements with coaches who support your journey.

  • Ages 10+
  • Personal progress tracking
  • Technique development
  • 2-3 sessions per week
Goal Achieving

Competitive Squad

For dedicated swimmers pursuing competitive goals. Intensive coaching in a supportive team environment where hard work is celebrated and your achievements matter.

  • All ages (ability-based)
  • Competition support
  • Advanced technique coaching
  • 3-4 sessions per week
Lifelong Swimming

Masters Squad

Adult swimmers at any level who want to stay fit, improve technique, or return to competitive swimming. Train in a supportive, judgment-free community.

  • Ages 18+
  • Flexible, supportive training
  • Fitness or competition options
  • 1-4 sessions per week
