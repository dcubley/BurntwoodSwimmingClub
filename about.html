Our Story

A Legacy Built in the Water

Founded in 1969, Burntwood Swimming Club has been at the heart of our community for over five decades. What began as a small group of passionate swimmers has grown into one of Staffordshire's most respected swimming clubs.

We believe that swimming is for everyone. Whether you're taking your first strokes or training for national competitions, our club provides a supportive environment where swimmers of all ages and abilities can thrive.

Our experienced coaches are dedicated to helping each member reach their personal goals, whether that's improving technique, building fitness, or competing at the highest levels.

What We Offer

Training for Every Goal

Recreational Swimming

Perfect for those who want to stay fit, improve their technique, and enjoy swimming in a friendly environment. Our recreational sessions focus on building confidence and stamina at your own pace.

  • Suitable for ages 8+
  • Focus on technique and fitness
  • Flexible training schedule
  • Supportive group atmosphere
Most Popular

Development Squads

For swimmers who want to take their abilities to the next level. Our development squads provide structured training with increasing intensity and technical focus.

  • Progressive skill development
  • Stroke technique refinement
  • Introduction to competitive swimming
  • Regular performance assessments

Competition Squad

Our elite squad for dedicated swimmers aiming to compete at regional, national, and beyond. Intensive training with our most experienced coaches.

  • High-performance coaching
  • Competition preparation
  • Advanced training techniques
  • Pathway to elite swimming
